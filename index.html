My Story

The engineer
behind the pipeline.

I've always been drawn to bringing structure to complexity — whether that's organizing an event where every detail needs to fall into place, or building a data pipeline where every record needs to land exactly where it should.

Outside of work, I enjoy planning and hosting events — decorating, organizing, and making sure everything comes together smoothly. That same instinct now shapes how I build reliable data systems.

My journey started at Accenture, where I worked with large datasets and messy, inconsistent data. I found myself genuinely enjoying the challenge of turning that complexity into something usable and dependable. That realization pushed me to pursue a Master's in Data Analytics Engineering at George Mason University.

Today, at Walmart Global Tech, I build the pipelines that support forecasting, personalization, and supply-chain teams. I care deeply about performance, reliability, and creating systems that make real decisions easier.

I'm currently open to full-time Data Engineer roles in the US. If you're building something that needs reliable, scalable data systems behind it — I'd love to connect.
